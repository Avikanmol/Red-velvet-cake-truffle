Red Velvet Cake Truffles 
Preparation 
i Prepare the red velvet cake accoding to your taste. 
ii In a bowl , crumble the red velvet cake.  
iii Mix in cream until smooth.
iv Roll into 1-2-Tbsp. - sized balls and chill.
v Melt white , milk and dark chocklate 
vi Roll the balls into the chocklate to coat evenly . Set aside.
vii Crush some candy and dry fruites . 
viii Decorate as per your wish .
